About me

Hello, I'm Alex Rivera, a passionate photographer based in Barcelona with over 7 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments. My journey began with an old film camera inherited from my grandfather, and it has evolved into a lifelong pursuit of visual storytelling.

My work

Blue Silence

A diver drifts into the vastness of the ocean, where time slows and sound fades away. The image captures human fragility within the endless calm of the sea.

Guardian of the Jungle

Hidden among leaves and shadows, a toucan watches in silence. Its vivid colors contrast with the deep green, reminding us that the jungle is always alive.

Last Light

Five silhouettes stand against the fading sun. The beach, the birds, and the darkening sky come together in a moment where day quietly gives way to night.

Yellow Past

An old yellow car breaks the stillness of the street. The photograph evokes nostalgia, frozen motion, and the beauty of things that refuse to be forgotten.
